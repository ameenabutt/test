      • Explain in your own words: How is explanatory data analysis different from exploratory data analysis?
       
      <font color='green'> **Exploratory data analysis**, is to explore the data to become familiar with it, and focuses on hypothesis formulation and digging through your data (data analysis) to find relationship and trends without a specific goal.
          
      **Explanatory visualization** focuses on the communication results, meaning that you have to explain outcomes or relationships of something specific you want to show an audiance / targetgroup by telling a story with your data. 
      </font>

      Exploratory data analysis, is to explore the data to become familiar with it, and focuses on hypothesis formulation and digging through your data (data analysis) to find relationship and trends without a specific goal.

      Explanatory visualization focuses on the communication results, meaning that you have to explain outcomes or relationships of something specific you want to show an audiance / targetgroup by telling a story with your data.
